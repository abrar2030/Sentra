Experience

I am a motivated Cloud & DevOps Engineer with over a year of hands-on experience in designing, deploying, and automating cloud-native infrastructures. My expertise lies in Kubernetes, CI/CD automation, containerization, and infrastructure-as-code (IaC) using Terraform and Helm. I specialize in streamlining cloud workflows, optimizing deployments, and implementing observability solutions to ensure high availability, security, and performance in modern IT environments. I am now seeking a Junior to Medior Cloud/DevOps Engineer role where I can contribute my skills while further expanding my expertise in cloud automation and scalability.

At Ericsson, I worked extensively with Kubernetes, Docker, Helm, and CI/CD pipelines to develop and optimize cloud-native solutions for scalable application deployments. I automated infrastructure provisioning and application deployment using GitHub Actions, Jenkins, and Helm, significantly reducing manual effort and improving deployment efficiency. My work also involved optimizing Kubernetes cluster performance, enhancing resource management, and strengthening observability using Prometheus and Grafana. Additionally, I contributed to security initiatives by implementing role-based access control (RBAC), network policies, and container security best practices.

Through project-based work, I have gained hands-on experience with AWS, leveraging cloud services to support containerized applications, implement storage solutions, and integrate security measures. I have successfully deployed cloud workloads with AWS EKS and automated infrastructure management using Terraform, ensuring scalability and cost efficiency. My ability to work across different cloud platforms enables me to build flexible, resilient architectures aligned with DevOps best practices.

I enjoy collaborating with teams to adopt cloud-native technologies, improve CI/CD workflows, and implement Kubernetes best practices. I have a strong technical foundation in container orchestration, infrastructure automation, and continuous deployment strategies that enhance software delivery and operational efficiency. My experience in multi-cloud environments allows me to develop scalable and efficient solutions that align with modern DevOps principles.

With a degree in Computer Science from Eötvös Loránd University and real-world experience in cloud automation, I am eager to take on new challenges that push the boundaries of cloud scalability, security, and efficiency. My goal is to drive innovation by enhancing deployment processes, automating workflows, and building resilient cloud infrastructures. I am actively seeking opportunities where I can apply my expertise in Kubernetes, CI/CD, and cloud technologies while continuing to grow in a dynamic DevOps environment.
